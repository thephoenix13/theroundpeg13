The idea

A round peg fits any hole. We named ourselves after the shape that adapts. — Why we exist

Most consultants are square pegs forced into round holes — rigid specialists making every problem look like theirs. We’re built the opposite way: a flexible network shaped to whatever you bring.

The collective spans multiple companies, disciplines, and geographies. When you engage us, you get the specific practitioners who’ve solved your problem before — not whoever happens to be on the bench.
