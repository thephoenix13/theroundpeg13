Our Story

We don’t fit. We adapt.

Most consultants are square pegs forced into round holes. We’re built the opposite way.

The name is the philosophy.

The classic idiom warns against putting a square peg in a round hole — forcing the wrong shape into the wrong space. We named ourselves after the opposite. We are the round peg: the shape that fits any hole, because we change shape for the brief.

The 13 is luck, signature, and the size of our founding circle.

A network, not a headcount.

Most firms sell you a roster of generalists. We don’t. Round Peg 13 is a collective of senior practitioners drawn from multiple companies — each with deep operational chops in a specific function or industry.

When you engage us, you get the specific people who’ve solved your specific problem before. Not the people we happen to have on the bench.

Practitioners, not slide-makers.

Everyone in the network has run the function they advise on. The person designing your performance management system has built two. The person hiring your VP Engineering used to be one. The person training your sales team carried a bag for ten years.

Our advice is grounded in “here’s what we did” — not “here’s a framework.”

One point of contact. Many capabilities.

You don’t want to manage five vendors for a single transformation. With us, one person owns the outcome — even when the work spans talent + L&D + tech + consulting.

We package, deliver, and the accountability sits with us.
