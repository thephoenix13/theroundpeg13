pgf-pal · AI assistant · prod-cluster-01
You
Why is query #4471 taking 2.3 seconds? It used to be under 100ms.
PGF Pal
Query #4471 is performing a sequential scan on the orders table (4.2M rows). Scan cost jumped from 0.3s to 2.3s as the table grew past 3M rows last week.
-- Suggested fix (safe on live cluster): CREATE INDEX CONCURRENTLY idx_orders_customer ON orders(customer_id); -- Expected: ~73% cost reduction -- Build time: ~4 minutes, no table lock
I'll monitor after deployment and alert if latency doesn't drop within 10 minutes.
You
Safe to run during business hours?
PGF Pal
Yes — CONCURRENTLY builds without locking the table. Connection pool is at 42%, well within safe range. You're good to go.

Common Questions

Honest answers to hard questions.

We already have a DBA. Why Postgres 1st?
+
Your DBA is valuable — and they need backup. We handle after-hours incidents, specialist deep-dives, and second opinions on architecture. Most clients report their DBA's productivity increases because we take over reactive firefighting.
Why not just use AWS RDS?
+
RDS gives you infrastructure. Not engineers who understand your query patterns, tune your indexes, or diagnose your specific regression. We complement cloud Postgres — most of our clients run on AWS or GCP.
How long does an Oracle migration take?
+
Depends on data volume and schema complexity. Most mid-market migrations take 4–12 weeks from assessment to cutover. We start with a free scoping call — you'll get a realistic estimate, not a template.
Can we start with monitoring only?
+
Yes. PGF WatchTower is available standalone. Many clients start there, get comfortable with the insights and our team, and expand to Managed Services. No pressure to commit upfront.
What makes you different from EDB or Percona?
+
EDB and Percona are US-based, priced in dollars, and built for global enterprise deals. We're India-based, IST-aligned, priced in rupees — without cutting corners on expertise or tooling quality.
