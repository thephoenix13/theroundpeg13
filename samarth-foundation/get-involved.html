Volunteer

We need people, not just money

Whether you are a doctor, a teacher, a logistics coordinator, or simply someone who wants to spend a Saturday in a village — we have a place for you.

Medical Volunteer

Specialists and general practitioners for Maha Arogya Shibir camps. Ophthalmologists, dentists, and gynaecologists are especially needed.

Education Support

Conduct workshops at rural schools. Help teachers with digital tools. Provide career guidance to Class 9–12 students from villages.

Field Operations

Help coordinate distribution camps, transport logistics, and village-level communication. Get hands-on in the field.

Remote Support

Content writing, design, data entry, grant writing, social media management. Contribute meaningfully from anywhere in the world.
