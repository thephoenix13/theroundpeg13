Our Story

Built from the ground up, for the ground.

Established in 2016 in Khed Taluka, Pune district, Samarth Foundation began with a simple conviction: that rural communities deserve the same quality of life as urban ones. Not charity — dignity.

"Every intervention begins with the village — not the boardroom."

Across education, healthcare, women’s empowerment, animal welfare, and environmental restoration, we partner with village leaders, school principals, and district health workers to co-design solutions that outlast our presence.
